Here are some tips given to you by MICA!

MedTips during Hot Weather/Summer

⚬ Stay hydrated. Drink lots of fluids to avoid dehydration.

⚬ Stay indoors during the hottest parts of the day.

⚬ Apply sunscreen with a high SPF to protect your skin from harmful UV rays.

⚬ Cooling off with a cool shower or bath can help lower your body temperature and provide relief from the heat.

⚬ Recognize the Signs of Heat Illness. Be aware of the signs of heat exhaustion and heatstroke.

MedTips during Rainy Seasons

- Stay Dry. Wear waterproof clothing and use umbrellas to keep yourself dry.

- Keep feet dry to avoid fungal infections.

- Avoid Stagnant Water. Prevent water accumulation to reduce mosquito breeding.

- Sanitize and Regularly clean commonly touched surfaces to kill germs.

- Wash hands frequently to prevent infections.

Healthy Travel Tips

- Get enough rest before and during travel.

- Wear supportive footwear for comfort.

- Choose nutritious meal options whenever possible.

- Walk around during layovers or breaks.

- Carry basic medical supplies for emergencies.
